# CareerToDo - Real-World Career Simulation Platform

## Basic Information
Name: CareerToDo
Website: https://careertodo.com
Category: EdTech, Career Development, Job Simulation Platform
Founded: Independent EdTech Project
Platform Type: Web-based career simulation and job readiness platform

## Platform Mission
CareerToDo helps students and job seekers bridge the gap between academic education and real-world professional work by providing practical job simulations.

Many graduates struggle to get jobs due to lack of real experience. CareerToDo allows learners to practice real workplace tasks in a safe, simulated environment before entering the job market.

Goal: Make students job-ready through hands-on learning.

## Core Value Proposition
Instead of learning only through tutorials or theory, users perform actual job tasks inside simulated business software environments.

Users gain practical experience with business workflows, decision making, and tool usage.

CareerToDo focuses on "learning by doing".

## Key Problems We Solve
- Students graduate without practical workplace experience
- Universities struggle to provide industry-level practical training
- Employers find it difficult to identify job-ready candidates
- Job seekers lack confidence in interviews due to lack of real task experience

CareerToDo solves these issues through simulation-based career training.

## Platform Structure

The platform includes:

- multiple simulation tools
- department-based task workflows
- guided task lists
- practical business scenarios
- simulated dashboards
- interactive user environments

Users complete real tasks instead of watching lessons.

## Learning Methodology

CareerToDo uses simulation-based learning:

1. Users explore a simulated business tool
2. Users receive real workplace tasks
3. Users perform actions inside the tool
4. Users build practical familiarity with workflows

This method improves confidence and job readiness.

## Unique Characteristics

CareerToDo is different from traditional online learning platforms.

Instead of focusing on video courses, the platform focuses on interactive simulations.

Key differentiators:

- task-based learning
- business software simulations

- experience-focused training
